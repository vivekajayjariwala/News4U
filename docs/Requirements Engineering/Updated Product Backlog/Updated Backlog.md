Note on the this folder and its contents

This folder is intended to hold the backlog, depicting the changes made in the additional window in Phase 5. 

The "Updated Product Backlog" shows our _entire_ backlog after Phase 4 (Sprint 2) and shows what items we actioned to move on. 

The "Simplified Product Backlog" shows the items that weren't complete and whether we decided to action them or whether we decided to abandon based on advice of our faculty advisor. 
Also has all epics removed for ease of reading. The "Notes" column writes what we did/why we chose not to complete. 

Generally, we decided to forego any of the feeback items on the product backlog as having a real feedback loop with our Gemini model seemed much too advanced for the scope, number of users, 
and the dataset of feeback available that were using our app. Our advisor also seemed to think our initial use of the model might be ambitious so this seemed reasonable to think it was out of scope.
